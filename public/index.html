About IICT
The Institute of Information and Communication Technology (IICT) at Khulna University of Engineering Technology is committed to nurturing talent, fostering innovation, and advancing research in technology. With a dynamic faculty, state-of-the-art facilities, and a vibrant student community, IICT is at the forefront of education and scientific discovery.
